Upgrade Policy

First off — thanks for being a CoinLogix user! You’re part of a growing group of collectors who like their software simple, private, and common-sense driven.

Minor Updates (2.x series)

If you purchase CoinLogix 2.1 (or any 2.x version), you’ll get all the small updates that come after it for free.

Minor updates might include:

  • New features
  • Small improvements
  • Bug fixes (if we find any — we try not to leave many!)

Examples:
If you bought version 2.1, you'll get 2.2, 2.3, 2.4, etc., no extra charge. Same with any new bugs. Yours. Free!
Simple. Fair. Done.

Major New Versions (3.x, 4.x, etc.)

A major upgrade (like jumping from version 2.x to 3.0) would be considered a brand-new product.

Why? Because at that point:

  • We’ll be adding big features
  • Rebuilding parts of the app
  • (And frankly) putting a lot more blood, sweat, and tears into making it even better.

Major new versions will require a new purchase.
(Hey, even the electric company charges you again every month.)

To sum it up:

Version Purpose
2.1 First commercial release. (done)
2.2 Small feature updates (free to 2.1 buyers).
2.3, 2.4 Tiny patches or tweaks (yep, still free).
3.0 Major overhaul. New paid product.

We believe in treating people fairly — and not squeezing every nickel out of you like a big corporate software giant.

Thanks again for being part of the CoinLogix family! You’re the reason small developers like us get to keep building better tools for real people.

P.S. — Don’t worry...

If you ever forget what version you have, just open CoinLogix and check the About tab. It's right there — no secret handshakes needed.
